MAHILL FOODS

Born in Nigeria in the summer of 2016 from our own desire to live healthier, we challenged ourselves to make the most nutritious and the most delicious yogurts on the planet. Following nearly years in development to achieve that ambition, we launched MAHILL FOODS and our range of Super Yogurts.

We're now on a mission to make it easier for people to eat better because healthier choices lead to more fulfilling lives. We believe that small improvements in our diet can have a profound impact on our quality of life.

Super Yogurt

100% natural and packed with protein, omega 3, essential vitamins and minerals,

our Super Yogurts are made with carefully selected 100% natural ingredients that are nutritionally powerful and absolutely delicious.

Our fruit is cold-pressed and never heat-treated. We refuse to use additives, preservatives, flavourings or nasties … because what we don’t put in matters just as much as what we do.

  • 100% Natural
  • 10g of Protein
  • 245mg Omega 3

4 Simple Ingredients

Our Super Yogurts are made using natures most delicious and nutritionally powerful ingredients ... strained BIO yogurt, cold-pressed organic fruit, blossom honey, and absolutely nothing else.

We refuse to use additives, preservatives, flavourings or nasties because what we don't put in matters just as much as what we do.
